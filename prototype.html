LouisGolf · Prototype

A caddy you can hear.

Turn sound on. On the hole, tap the map to place your ball (the yardage updates), then hold the orb and talk to Louis — it's the only button you need. Ask for the play, tell him "I'm in the water," or say "next hole," and he responds. Tap the score in the corner to see the card. (Voice works in Chrome/Safari; a tap still gives the read.)

🔊Sound on

Pick the smoothest voice your device offers. On iPhone, the "Siri" or "Enhanced/Premium" voices sound best (Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices to add them).
